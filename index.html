PROCESS

How we ship work

Clear stages, written scope and milestone-based delivery — so you always know what’s going on.

1 · Discover & define
We run a short discovery with you to understand the problem, users, constraints and success metrics.
2 · Design & plan
We sketch user flows, wireframes and UI. In parallel we define tech stack, milestones, and timelines.
3 · Build in focused sprints
Development happens in short cycles with regular demos. You see functional progress, not just promises.
4 · Test, deploy, support
We handle QA, handover, deployment and optional maintenance plans for ongoing fixes and small enhancements.
Engagement options
Fixed scope projects, retainer-style support or dedicated resources through staff augmentation.
Money & terms
Typical structure: 30% to start, 30% mid-way, 40% on final delivery — with everything written into a simple MSA + SOW.
Compliance
MSME + GST registered; contracts, NDAs and clear invoicing for Indian and global clients.
