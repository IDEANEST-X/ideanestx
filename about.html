ABOUT

About IDEANEST X

IDEANEST X is a hybrid studio focused on building apps, websites and game experiences. We sit at the intersection of software development and game design — which means we care about both performance and how it feels to use.

Who we are

We’re a small, focused team based in Tamil Nadu, working with clients across India and beyond. We’re comfortable being your full development team or an extended arm to your existing team.

  • Product mindset – we think in journeys, not just pages and screens.
  • Tech-first but design-aware – we want things to feel good and run fast.
  • Honest about constraints – if your scope or budget is unrealistic, we’ll say it.

Registrations & compliance

IDEANEST X is MSME-registered and GST-ready. As we grow, we’re structuring things to be private-limited ready so that investment, equity and complex contracts become easier.

  • MSME registration for easier B2B and government alignment.
  • GST-compliant invoicing and returns.
  • Separate current account for business finances.

How we like to work

  • Clear written scope before we start building.
  • Regular check-ins, not just “see you at launch”.
  • Transparent pricing & milestone-based payments.

Future-facing

From government tenders and platforms like GeM to game grants and publisher deals, IDEANEST X is being built with the long term in mind — not as a one-off freelance profile.
